Filipino Egg Tarts

Description

A sweet and creamy filipino dessert, these egg tarts are quick and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 30 small pastry shells
  • 8 egg yolks
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix the egg yolks, sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk and vanilla extract together in a large bowl
  2. Scoop the mixture into a pastry shell just below full
  3. Bake in oven for approximately 20 mins or until mixture sets but is still jiggly
  4. After baking, let egg tarts rest in room temperature for 30 mins
