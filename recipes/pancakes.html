Sour Cream Pancakes

Description

This pancake recipe is light and fluffy, and also includes a a secret ingredient: sour cream

Ingredients

    Dry:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/8 cup sugar
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1tsp baking powder
    • Wet:
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1tbsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix all wet ingredients together, it's okay if there are clumps
  2. In a seperate bowl mix dry ingredients together
  3. Pour the dry ingredients into wet ingredients then mix thoroughly
