Lumpia Shanghai

Description

Lumpia is a popular filipino dish, mini bite size eggrolls, usually served with either chili or plum sauce!

Ingredients

  • 3lbs ground pork
  • 2 bunches of green onions
  • 2 carrots
  • 8 cloves of garlic
  • 1 egg for egg wash
  • 200 small egg roll wrappers
  • Small pot of oil to deep fry

Steps

  1. Using a foor processor, chop green onions, carrots and garlic
  2. Mix the green onions, carrots and garlic with the pork in a large bowl
  3. Mix 1 egg with 2tbsp of water in a small bowl, we will use this to seal the wrappers
  4. Take 1tbsp of the pork mixture and scoop it onto 1 lumpia wrapper
  5. Tightly wrap the mixture and use the egg wash to close the roll
  6. Repeat steps until all wrappers are used
    • You can freeze any lumpia you won't be deepfrying in a freezer bag
  7. Deep fry the lumpia in hot oil for about 5-10 mins or until golden brown
